The Investment Framework

The Pre-Construction Advantage: How Silverdome Clients Compound 40–80% Every 3–4 Years

1

Enter branded, under-construction projects at pre-launch or early-phase pricing

Typically 15–25% below what the surrounding resale market already transacts at. Deploy only 20–30% upfront. The balance is staggered across 3–5 years of construction — protecting your liquidity.

2

Allow the phased pricing mechanism to drive appreciation

Smart developers launch in phases — Phase 1 at ₹23k/sq ft, Phase 2 at ₹25k/sq ft, Phase 3 at ₹35k/sq ft. Each new phase sets a higher price floor that lifts all early investors. Silverdome positions clients at Phase 1 or Phase 2 entries.

3

By Year 3–4, your asset has appreciated 40–50% on the total value

Driven by construction progress, infrastructure completion, and the surrounding market catching up. Exit at a premium, hold for rental income, or rotate into the next cycle with Silverdome's guidance. This playbook converted ₹5 CR into ₹9–10 CR for Silverdome clients who entered Dwarka Expressway projects in 2021–22.
